Take a trip to Seoul!

Annyeong haseyo! Seoul, pronounced 'Soul' to the modern western english speaker, is the capital of South Korea and largest city in the country with a population of nearly 10 million people! The name of the capital has been changed several times throughout history. It was most recently renamed Seoul in 1945 after World War 2. I chose Seoul due to the increasing international attention given to the country thanks to the k-pop scene, their cinema, their skincare, etc. From their cuisine to their beautiful landscapes and rich history, I definitely think that this is a country you don't want to look over should you decide to travel to east Asia.

Fun Facts

Three kingdoms -- Baekje, Goguryeo, and Silla (BCE 57 – CE 688) all fought over Korea's capital.

1/5 to 1/3 of the women in Seoul have gone under the knife for at least one cosmetic procedure.

In Korea, babies are considered one years old at birth.
