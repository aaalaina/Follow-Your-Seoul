Activities and Festivities

Lotte World

For all ages!

Address: Lotte World Mall, 300, Olympic-ro, Songpa-gu, Seoul Price: Tickets cost $59,000 won or roughly 50 USD for adults!

There's no chance you'll be bored at the biggest theme park in South Korea! With over 7 million visitors a year this is easily one of the most popular tourist hotspots in Korea. It has everything: amusement park rides, a shopping mall, aquarium, and food center! If you're looking to have a good time you should definitely stop here for a few hours.

Boseong Green Tea Festival

For all ages

775, Nokcha-ro, Boseong-gun, Jeollanam-do, Seoul Price: Free entry! And the only tea plantation that is open to tourists.

This plantation is known for its gorgeous fields of tea leaves that span over 5,000,000 square meters. Every year in May, a tea festival is hosted with various green tea based foods, crafts, and games. You can even pick a few tea leaves!

Seoul Lantern Festival

For all ages

6F, Cheonggyecheon-ro 85, Jongno-gu, Seoul Price: Free of charge! The lanterns are for the general public to see.

East Asian countries tend to have a rich history with lanterns, South Korea being no exception. This festival is one of the city's most attended celebrations with over 2 million people attending each year in November!

Dongdaemon Night Market

No age limit but I'd suggest only adults.

288, Jong-ro, Jongno-gu, Seoul Price: Varies

With more than 26 shopping malls, 30,000 specialty shops, and 50,000 manufacturers, you'll definitely pass a shop that's just your style! If you get a little hungry while shopping for clothing, feel free to take a break and grab a snack at nearby food markets or street food vendors.
